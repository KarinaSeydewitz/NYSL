About 

 
         MISSION
        To support young athletes living in Chicago´s Northside Neighborhoods,who have an interest in 
        learning and playing soccer, with opportunities to learn and practice skills related to the 
        game of soccer, specifically those skills around team cooperation and good sportsmanship.
    
        VISSION
        The Northside Youth Soocer League aspires to developed strong, well-rounded, and mindful
        athletes trough the building of character, self-discipline, and learship.
    
        GENERAL INFORMATION
        The Northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing
        in Chicago´s Northside Neighborhoods an enviroment in which to learn and play soccer.
        To be a member of NYSL, you must be between the ages  of 4-12 and reside in a Chicago´s Northside
        Neighborhoods. NYSL is ran by a  small full-time staff, and relies on the generous volunteer
        time of parents and previous league members.
